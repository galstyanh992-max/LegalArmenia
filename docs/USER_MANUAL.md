## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 What is Legal AI Platform?

Legal AI Platform is a digital tool designed to help legal professionals work more efficiently. The platform provides access to a comprehensive legal knowledge base, AI-powered document analysis, and automated document generation.

### 1.2 What Problems Does It Solve?

- **Quick access to legal information**: Search through codes, laws, and court decisions in seconds
- **Document analysis**: Get AI-powered insights on legal cases
- **Document creation**: Generate legal documents using templates
- **Case organization**: Keep all case materials in one place
- **Audio processing**: Transcribe court recordings and interviews

### 1.3 Who Is This Platform For?

- Lawyers and attorneys
- Legal assistants and paralegals
- Law students
- Legal researchers
- Anyone needing access to legal information

### 1.4 Important Disclaimer

> ⚠️ **IMPORTANT NOTICE**
>
> Legal AI is an informational tool and does NOT provide official legal advice. 
> All AI-generated content is for reference purposes only and may contain errors.
> 
> **Always consult a licensed attorney for legal matters.**
> 
> The platform does not replace professional legal counsel and should not be relied upon for making legal decisions.

## 4. Search Functionality

### 4.1 Using the Search Assistant

The Search Assistant uses AI to understand your query and find relevant documents.

**How to Search:**

1. Locate the Search Assistant on your Dashboard
2. Type your question or keywords
3. Press Enter or click the search button
4. Wait for results to appear

**Example Searches:**
- "Article 55 of the Criminal Code"
- "Property rights in civil law"
- "Termination of employment contract"
- "Inheritance rules"

### 4.2 Search Tips for Better Results

| Tip | Example |
|-----|---------|
| Use specific terms | "unjust enrichment" instead of "money" |
| Include article numbers | "Article 178 Criminal Code" |
| Use legal terminology | "force majeure" not "unexpected event" |
| Try both languages | Search in Armenian or Russian |

### 4.3 Understanding Search Results

Each search result shows:

- **Title** — Document or article name
- **Snippet** — Relevant excerpt from the text
- **Source** — Origin of the document
- **Category** — Classification of the content

### 4.4 If No Results Are Found

If your search returns no results:

1. Check spelling of search terms
2. Try different keywords or synonyms
3. Use broader search terms
4. Try searching in a different language
5. Contact administrator if the document should exist

## 5. AI Legal Chat

### 5.1 What is AI Legal Chat?

The AI Legal Chat allows you to ask questions about your cases and receive AI-powered analysis. The AI can:

- Analyze case facts
- Identify applicable laws
- Suggest legal arguments
- Review evidence
- Highlight procedural considerations

### 5.2 How to Use AI Chat

1. Open a case from your Cases list
2. Navigate to the AI Analysis section
3. Select the type of analysis you need:
   - **Defense perspective** — Arguments for the defense
   - **Prosecution perspective** — Prosecution viewpoint
   - **Judge perspective** — Neutral judicial analysis
   - **Evidence analysis** — Review of evidence
   - **Procedural review** — Process and timeline issues
   - **Rights analysis** — Constitutional and human rights

4. Click "Run Analysis"
5. Wait for the AI to generate the response
6. Review the analysis carefully

### 5.3 Difference Between Search and AI Chat

| Feature | Search | AI Chat |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Purpose | Find existing documents | Analyze your case |
| Input | Keywords | Case details |
| Output | Document excerpts | Custom analysis |
| Speed | Instant | Takes time to process |
| Accuracy | High (returns actual text) | Variable (AI interpretation) |

### 5.4 Limitations of AI Responses

> ⚠️ **Important Limitations:**
>
> - AI may make mistakes or miss important details
> - AI cannot access information outside its training data
> - AI analysis is not a substitute for legal expertise
> - Always verify AI suggestions with actual legal sources
> - Recent law changes may not be reflected

### 5.5 Best Practices for AI Chat

1. **Provide complete information** — More details lead to better analysis
2. **Be specific** — Vague questions get vague answers
3. **Include relevant facts** — Dates, parties, amounts
4. **Specify jurisdiction** — Mention which court or instance
5. **Review critically** — Always verify AI output
6. **Use multiple perspectives** — Run different analysis types

## 6. Case Management

### 6.1 Creating a New Case

1. Click "New Case" on the Dashboard or Cases page
2. Fill in required information:
   - Case title
   - Case number
   - Case type (Criminal, Civil, Administrative)
   - Description
   - Court name
   - Court date (if known)
3. Set priority level (Low, Medium, High, Urgent)
4. Click "Create Case"

### 6.2 Uploading Case Files

1. Open your case
2. Go to the "Files" section
3. Click "Upload File" or drag and drop files
4. Supported formats:
   - PDF documents
   - Images (JPG, PNG, TIFF, WebP)
   - Audio files (for transcription)
5. Wait for upload to complete

### 6.3 Using OCR for Scanned Documents

If you upload scanned documents or images:

1. The system will automatically perform OCR (text recognition)
2. Wait for processing to complete
3. Review the extracted text for accuracy
4. Edit if needed (especially for handwritten text)

### 6.4 Case Status Management

Track your case progress with status updates:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Open | New case, work not started |
| In Progress | Active work ongoing |
| Pending | Waiting for external action |
| Closed | Case concluded |
| Archived | Stored for reference |

## 7. Document Generation

The platform can generate:

- Legal complaints and petitions
- Court applications
- Motions and requests
- Appeals

- ECHR applications
- General legal letters

## 8. Audio Transcription

### 8.1 Supported Audio Formats

- MP3
- WAV
- M4A
- OGG
- WebM

### 8.2 How to Transcribe Audio

1. Navigate to "Audio Transcriptions"
2. Click "Upload Audio"
3. Select your audio file
4. Wait for processing (time depends on file length)
5. Review the transcription
6. Edit any errors
7. Mark as reviewed when complete

### 8.3 Transcription Quality Tips

For best results:

- Use clear audio recordings
- Minimize background noise
- Ensure speakers are audible
- Longer pauses may be misinterpreted
- Multiple speakers may need manual identification

### 8.4 Reviewing Transcriptions

After transcription:

1. Read through the entire text
2. Correct any recognition errors
3. Add speaker labels if needed
4. Check timestamps
5. Mark as "Reviewed" when satisfied
