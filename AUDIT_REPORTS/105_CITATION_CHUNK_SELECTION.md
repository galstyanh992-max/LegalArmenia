# 105 — Citation chunk selection

Citation selection uses trusted provision boundaries, version/status eligibility, a minimal primary passage, and only proven adjacent chunks with the same document/provision and consecutive chunk index. Virtual spans preserve original chunk IDs.

Canonical formatting never invents page, provision, URL, or authority. Unknown/repealed warnings remain structured and mandatory for their scopes.

Evidence: `supabase/functions/_shared/citation-chunk-selection.ts`, `supabase/functions/_shared/legal-citation-metadata.ts`.
